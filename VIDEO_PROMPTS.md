# Prompts de Video para StarbizAcademy (Veo 3.1)

Este documento contiene los prompts optimizados para generar los "micro-videos" (loops de fondo) para la web.

**Instrucciones Generales para Veo 3.1:**
*   **Estilo:** Cinematográfico, Futurista, High-Tech, Limpio.
*   **Colores Clave:** Cyan (Cian), Purple (Morado), Orange (Naranja).
*   **Movimiento:** Suave, lento (slow motion), sin cortes bruscos (loopable).

---

## 1. Sección: PROGRAMAS (Panel Lateral "CEO Junior")
**Ubicación:** El recuadro vertical a la derecha en la sección de programas.
**Formato Recomendado:** **9:16 (Vertical)**

### Opción A: El Joven Visionario (Abstracto)
> **Prompt:** Cinematic vertical shot, close-up of a futuristic glass tablet held by a young hand, displaying glowing holographic code and business graphs in cyan and orange neon. The background is a blurred modern workspace at night with bokeh city lights. High-tech atmosphere, crystal clear resolution, slow smooth motion, photorealistic, 8k.

### Opción B: Arquitectura Digital
> **Prompt:** Vertical video, abstract 3D animation of a digital futuristic city rising from a grid. Buildings forming from data streams. Glowing lines of code ascending like skyscrapers. Color palette: Deep black background with neon cyan and electric purple highlights. Smooth continuous upward motion, seamless loop, volumetric lighting, unreal engine 5 style.

---

## 2. Sección: 7 INTELIGENCIAS (Fondos de Tarjetas)
**Ubicación:** Efecto hover o fondo sutil para las tarjetas.
**Formato Recomendado:** **9:16 (Vertical)**

### Inteligencia Tecnológica (IA)
> **Prompt:** Vertical close-up of a high-tech quantum processor chip. Pulses of electrical energy flowing through gold and cyan circuits. Macro photography style, shallow depth of field, metallic textures, glowing nodes, slow elegant energy flow, cinematic lighting.

### Inteligencia Financiera
> **Prompt:** Vertical animation of abstract digital financial data. Golden streams of numbers and rising geometric graphs forming a tree shape. Dark background, gold and emerald green glowing accents. Smooth floating particles, prestige feeling, high fidelity 3D render.

### Inteligencia Mental (Neurociencia)
> **Prompt:** Vertical abstract animation of a neural network. Glowing purple and white synapses firing and connecting in a dark void. Representation of a digital brain, elegant, fluid motion, mysterious and powerful, volumetric fog, 8k resolution.

---

## 3. Sección: ECOSISTEMA (Fondo de Comunidad)
**Ubicación:** Fondo detrás de la sección "Comunidades" o "Padres 3.0".
**Formato Recomendado:** **16:9 (Landscape/Horizontal)**

### Conexión Global
> **Prompt:** Cinematic wide shot of a digital globe rotating slowly. Lines of connection arching across continents representing a global network. Nodes lighting up in orange and cyan. Deep space background with subtle stars. High-tech enterprise aesthetic, clean, minimalist, data visualization style.

### Colaboración Futura (Siluetas)
> **Prompt:** Wide cinematic shot, silhouettes of diverse young people standing in front of a massive futuristic window looking out at a neon cyber-city. They are gesturing at holographic screens. Backlit, mysterious, inspiring mood. Colors: Deep blue shadows with warm orange horizon light. Slow motion, 4k.

---

## 4. Sección: HERO (Fondo Principal - Opcional)
**Ubicación:** Reemplazo del Starfield actual (si se desea video real).
**Formato Recomendado:** **16:9 (Landscape/Horizontal)**

### Viaje Estelar (Warp Speed)
> **Prompt:** First-person view of traveling through a deep space nebula. Soft clouds of purple and cyan gas passing by. Occasional bright stars zooming past. A subtle orange glow in the center distance (the destination). Hypnotic, peaceful but fast, cosmic scale, cinematic vfx, 8k wallpaper style.
